Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 03, 2025

1. Who We Are

Kiff-kach is a personal finance tracking application developed by MedBen. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Contact: kiffkach@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

Registration Data

  • Email Address: to create your account and contact you
  • Password: encrypted and secured
  • Social Login Information: if you use Google/Apple (name, email, profile picture)

Financial Data

  • Transactions: amounts, categories, dates (stored locally on your device)
  • Budgets and Goals: your personal financial settings
  • Preferences: application settings

Consent Data

  • Email Preferences: your choice regarding marketing communications
  • Consent Date: timestamp of your choices
  • Consent Source: where you gave your agreement

3. How We Use Your Data

Application Operation

  • Provide financial tracking services
  • Synchronize your data across devices
  • Improve user experience

Communications (with your explicit consent)

  • Financial Tips: advice to optimize your budget
  • Product Updates: new features
  • Educational Content: financial articles and guides

Maximum Frequency: 2 emails per week

Unsubscribe: possible at any time

4. Data Sharing

We NEVER sell your personal data.

Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): secure data storage
  • Mailchimp: marketing email management (only if you consent)
  • Authentication Providers: Google, Apple (for login)

Legal Situations

We may disclose your data if:

  • Required by law
  • Necessary to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

Technical Measures

  • Encryption: all data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: secure access via password or biometrics
  • Monitoring: continuous access monitoring

Organizational Measures

  • Limited data access by staff
  • Regular security training
  • Periodic security audits

6. Your Rights

GDPR Rights

  • Right of Access: request a copy of your data
  • Right of Rectification: correct your data
  • Right to Erasure: delete your account
  • Right to Portability: export your data
  • Right to Object: refuse marketing processing
  • Right of Withdrawal: cancel your consent

7. Data Retention

  • Active Account: data retained as long as your account exists
  • Deleted Account: data erased within 30 days
  • Marketing Data: deleted immediately after unsubscription
  • Security Logs: retained for maximum 12 months

8. Minors

Our application is not intended for children under 16 years old. If you are under 16, do not use our service.

9. Changes

We will inform you of important changes by:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if you have consented)
  • Updating the date at the top of this page
